LANDSKABES

Landscapes are scenic areas where a variety of edible plants and mushrooms thrive. These areas can include forests, meadows, coastal regions, and fields. Foraging in these landscapes provides a unique connection to nature and the opportunity to harvest fresh, natural ingredients for cooking.

INGREDIENTS

Ingredients for foraging include a wide range of edible plants, herbs, and mushrooms found growing wild in nature. These natural ingredients are filled with flavor, nutrition, and often medicinal properties.

RECIPES

Recipes for foraging are creative and varied, leveraging the unique flavor profiles of gathered ingredients. These recipes emphasize both sustainability and nutrition, as foraged ingredients are often packed with vitamins and minerals.

WHAT IS FORAGING?

Find area

When you embark on foraging, it's important to start by finding the right area.

Collect

When you've found the perfect spot, it's time to start collecting. First and foremost, you need to learn to identify edible plants and mushrooms.

Preparation

Cleaning is an important part of the process. Rinse your plants and mushrooms thoroughly under cold water to remove soil, insects, or other impurities.

Cooking

Now you're ready to cook with your foraged ingredients. It's important to be creative in the kitchen. Try different recipes and experiment with your foraged ingredients.
